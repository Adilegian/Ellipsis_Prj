Laser by JuniorPro.
User's Manual

Laser is a LowPoly fully game ready unity asset with 2 functionalities
1. Laser Beam,
2. Flash Light.


How to use:

Just drag the prefab (which can be found in the root folder) named "Military Laser" in your scene.
Use Mouse1 button for Laser beam and Mouse2 button for Flash Light.
There are 2 buttons on laser model for Laser and Flash, which user can define accordingly.

Also there is a pointer image in the OtherPrefabs folder indicating the laser's hit which users can use.
